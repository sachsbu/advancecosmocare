What is Laser Hair Reduction?

Laser Hair Reduction is a safe, non-invasive procedure that uses concentrated light energy to target hair follicles, reducing hair growth over time.

Unlike temporary hair removal methods such as waxing or shaving, this treatment selectively affects the melanin in hair follicles while fully preserving the surrounding skin tissue, leading to lasting smoothness.
