What is HydraFacial?

HydraFacial is a non-invasive, multi-step skin treatment that combines deep cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, hydration, and antioxidant protection in a single session.

Using patented vortex-fusion technology, it gently clears away impurities, unclogs pores, and infuses clinical-grade serums containing peptides and hyaluronic acid into the skin, leaving you with an instant, noticeable glow.
