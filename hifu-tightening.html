Advanced Energy-Based Lift

HIFU & Skin Tightening

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is a revolutionary non-surgical procedure that lifts, tightens, and contours the face, jawline, and neck using focused sound waves.

By targeting the deep structural layers of the skin—the same layers addressed in surgical facelifts—HIFU stimulates the natural production of new collagen and elastin, delivering long-lasting firming results with zero downtime.

HIFU Treatment Indications

✓

Loose or Sagging Skin

Gradually lifts and tightens sagging areas around the cheeks and eyebrows.

✓

Jawline Definition

Contours the lower face to create a sharper, more sculpted, and defined jawline.

✓

Double Chin Reduction

Firms submental skin and helps refine the contour underneath the chin.

✓

Neck Tightening

Tightens loose neck skin and helps smooth neck lines (often called tech neck).

✓

Fine Lines & Wrinkles

Smoothes out mild expression lines, wrinkles, and crow's feet for a younger look.

✓

Mild Skin Laxity on the Face

Provides an overall firming boost to restore skin bounce and general elasticity.

How Our Treatment Works

Step 1

Clinical Assessment

We evaluate your skin thickness, assess the degree of laxity, and map target depths.

Step 2

HIFU Treatment

Focused ultrasound energy is delivered to the deeper layers of the skin, where it stimulates collagen production without damaging the skin's surface.

Step 3

Natural Results

As new collagen forms over the following weeks and months, the skin gradually becomes firmer, tighter, and more lifted.
