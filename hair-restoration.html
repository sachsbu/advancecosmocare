Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the treatment process, comfort, and efficacy.

Based on your clinical assessment, your customized treatment plan may include Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy, Growth Factor Concentrate (GFC) therapy, medical treatments, specialized scalp therapies, nutritional support, and a personalized hair care routine.
PRP and GFC therapies utilize natural growth factors harvested from your own blood. These growth factors are delivered directly to the hair roots to stimulate dormant follicles, improve blood supply, and promote thicker, healthier hair growth.
Generally, 4 to 6 sessions spaced 4 weeks apart are recommended to see visible improvements. Results such as reduced hair fall and improved hair texture can often be noticed after the 2nd or 3rd session.
There is virtually zero downtime. You can resume your daily routine immediately after the session, although we advise avoiding heavy workouts or washing your hair for a few hours post-treatment.
