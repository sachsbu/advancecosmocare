Understanding Acne & Acne Scars

Acne develops due to excess oil (sebum) production, clogged pores, bacterial activity, and inflammation. If left untreated, it may lead to pigmentation changes (dark spots), textural irregularities, and permanent scarring.

Our treatment approach focuses on controlling active acne breakouts, reducing underlying inflammation, preventing future breakouts, and improving the appearance of existing scars through advanced collagen stimulation.

Targeted Treatment Goals

  • ✓ Sebum Regulation & Pore Unclogging
  • ✓ Eliminating Acne-Causing Bacteria
  • ✓ Fading Post-Inflammatory Erythema (Redness)
  • ✓ Collagen Induction & Resurfacing for Scars

Our Treatment Steps

Step 1

Comprehensive Skin Assessment

A detailed consultation is conducted to evaluate:

  • Acne severity and type (inflammatory, cystic, comedonal)
  • Scar pattern and depth (ice-pick, boxcar, rolling scars)
  • Skin sensitivity, hydration levels, and phototype
  • Lifestyle factors, diet, stress, and previous treatments
Step 2

Personalized Treatment Planning

Based on the assessment, a customized treatment plan combining clinical interventions and home care strategies is designed to address individual concerns and achieve optimal results.

Step 3

Active Acne Management

Targeted therapies are deployed to control active flares, decrease inflammation, and regulate oil:

  • Professional acne peels
  • Deep cleansing treatments
  • Sebum regulation therapies
  • Anti-inflammatory skin protocols
  • Medical-grade skincare recommendations
Step 4

Acne Scar Correction

Depending on scar type and skin condition, advanced procedures are recommended to stimulate collagen production and restore structural integrity:

  • Chemical Peel Therapy
  • Microneedling Treatments
  • Collagen Induction Procedures
  • Skin Resurfacing Protocols
  • Combination Rejuvenation Treatments
Step 5

Recovery & Maintenance

Following treatment, patients receive a personalized skincare regimen along with vital guidance on sun protection, soothing hydration, and routine maintenance sessions to preserve and enhance results.
